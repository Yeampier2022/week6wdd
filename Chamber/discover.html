Gilbert Arizona Stats

Arizona is a state in the southwestern region of the US known for its hot, dry climate, beautiful desert landscapes, and natural wonders such as the Grand Canyon. It has a population of around 7.5 million people and a diverse economy. Popular tourist destinations include the Grand Canyon, Sedona, and Monument Valley.

It has been days since your last visit.

Antelope Canyon

Antelope Canyon, known for its stunning natural beauty, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Arizona. This slot canyon is located on Navajo land near Page, Arizona.

The Wave Deserves a Top Spot

In the heart of the Arizona desert lies a geological wonder known as The Wave. Hikers who make the trek to this unique formation are rewarded with awe-inspiring views of striated sandstone curves that seem to have been sculpted by waves. The hike is a little over 6 miles round trip and the best time to go is during spring or fall when the weather is most temperate.

White Pocket is a Hidden Gem in Arizona

One of the most unique places to visit in Arizona is White Pocket. This stunning spot is located in the Vermilion Cliffs National Monument and is well worth the trek for anyone looking for an off-the-beaten-path adventure.

Horseshoe Bend Has Stunning Views

An Arizona Bucket List wouldn’t be complete without a visit to Horseshoe Bend. The Horseshoe Bend hike is an easy 15 minute walk to the canyon’s edge where you’ll see the horseshoe shaped meander of the Colorado River with 1,000 foot canyon walls surrounding.
